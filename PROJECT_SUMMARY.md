## Key Metrics

- **Alphabet Size**: 29 characters (a-z, space, comma, period)
- **Sequence Length**: Configurable (default 100-200)
- **Training Methods**: Pseudo-likelihood, KL-gradient
- **Sampling**: Block Gibbs with temperature control
- **Pattern Detection**: Bigrams, motifs, character clusters
